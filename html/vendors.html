Vendors

Here at MadeSimple, we do our utmost best to help small businesses grow. At first we wanted to provide our community with a list of local producers but at the end of the day we couldn't see it having any real impact.

So instead we turned to what is our current solution: Buy your groceries from small businesses, our partners, and deliver it straight to you.

At the end of the day, you're happy because grocery shopping has become that much easier, our partners are happy because they see an increase in sales, and lastly we are happy because of you.

Our Standards

Just so you know, we don't just let anyone partner with us.

We have a set standard to ensure that everything we deliver is of the highest quality.

This means, visiting farms, taste-tests, interviewing the producers and somtimes we even interview the animals too.

I mean nobody wants mistreated animals, right?

It Has To Be Ethical

Made Simple only works with vendors who are bound to an ethical moral code. That means vendors partake in honest and respectful behavior during interactions with others, animals, and our planet.
Our vendors have made a promise to always do the right thing, it is that simple.

It Has To Be Transparent

Transparency is important. We partner with vendors who have clear agendas and are open to sharing information about their business practices at any point in time to encourage collaboration, cooperation, and inclusive decision-making.

All contracted vendors are individually and spontaneously evaluated by Made Simpleâ€™s quality assurance officers. QA is an important step in our partnership process. It ensures that our customers receive clean and hygienic groceries.

It Has To Be Local

In efforts to support local, small businesses, Made Simple partners with locally-sourced food vendors only. We believe that if we support our local market, they will sustain us with groceries of the best quality.
It is that simple.
